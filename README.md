# Fashion Brand Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## Description

This project is an Excel-based dashboard. It helps analyze a fashion brands performance. The dashboard shows sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, delivery efficiency and regional revenue distribution. It turns raw business data into insights. This helps make better decisions.

## Purpose

The dashboard gives a business overview of the fashion brand. It tracks sales performance and customer buying patterns. It identifies products and regions. It also evaluates delivery and customer satisfaction.

## Key Features

### Executive KPIs
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Revenue
- Average Rating
- Average Delivery Time

### Sales Trend Analysis

- Last 13 weeks performance
- Quantity and revenue trend comparison
- Peak sales periods

### Customer Behavior Analysis
- How customers buy
- Buying patterns by gender
- Sales analysis across channel:
App
Instagram
Partner App
Target.com
Website

### Product Performance Analysis

- Top products by gender
- Popular product categories
- Best-selling items like:
T-Shirts
Sneakers
Bikinis
Sundresses
Hoodies & Sweatshirts
Casual Dresses
Tote Bag
Baseball Cap

### Geographic Revenue Analysis

- Top 10 countries/regions by revenue
- Location-wise sales contribution
- Revenue concentration
  
### Delivery Performance Analysis
- Time taken to ship orders
- Shipping efficiency
- Fast delivery trends

### Business Insights
- Revenue comes from a few top regions
- App and Website drive sales
- Fast delivery helps
- Sales depend on a few products
- Customers are satisfied, with a 4.0 average rating

- Pivot Tables

## Outcome

This dashboard shows fashion brand performance. It uses Excel for business intelligence data analysis and reporting.
